Weekly Timesheet

Submit every Friday for the full work week (Monday → Friday). Smallest unit: 0.0625 days = 30 min (0.125 = 1h, 0.25 = 2h, 0.5 = half day, 1 = full day). Total must equal 5 days. If you log less than 5, the gap auto-fills to Production interne. If you log more than 5, submit is blocked. Resubmit anytime — latest overrides previous for the same week.
